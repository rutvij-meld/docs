Meld supports two types of ACH transactions: Payins (debits) and Payouts (credits). This page explains the prerequisites and the high-level sequence for taking your first ACH transaction. It is intended for developers who already have an API key and want to understand the full ACH flow before integrating.

## Definitions

- **Payin** (aka Debit or Charge): money moves from a buyer to a merchant.
- **Payout** (aka Credit): money moves from a merchant to a buyer.
- **Refund**: a special type of Payout. To issue a full or partial refund of a Payin, initiate a Payout with the desired amount. To reverse a Payout, initiate a Payin for the same amount.
- **Cashout**: moving money from a merchant account on the provider to the merchant's checking account at their bank. Treat this as a Payout.

## Prerequisites

<Info>
- A configured ACH-capable service provider in your dashboard (see [Supported Service Providers](/docs/payments-stack/fiat-supported-service-providers)).
- A merchant account set up with the service provider — contact Meld for help here.
- A verified buyer bank account, obtained via Meld's Bank Linking [Account Verification](/docs/bank-linking/bank-linking-quickstart/account-verification) flow with the [IDENTIFIERS](/docs/bank-linking/bank-linking-quickstart/bank-linking-products/account-identifiers) and [OWNERS](/docs/bank-linking/bank-linking-quickstart/bank-linking-products/account-owners) products requested.
- A Meld customer for the buyer (see [Creating a Customer](/docs/payments-stack/fiat-payment-quickstart/making-ach-payments/creating-a-payments-customer)).
</Info>

## Steps to make an ACH transaction

1. **Account verification** — Use Meld's Bank Linking products to verify the user's bank account. This process is known as [Account Verification](/docs/bank-linking/bank-linking-quickstart/account-verification) and requires the [IDENTIFIERS](/docs/bank-linking/bank-linking-quickstart/bank-linking-products/account-identifiers) (account and routing number) and [OWNERS](/docs/bank-linking/bank-linking-quickstart/bank-linking-products/account-owners) (account owner's name) products.
2. **Create a customer** — For each ACH transaction you need a Meld customer to represent the buyer. If the buyer is new, create one by following the [steps here](/docs/payments-stack/fiat-payment-quickstart/making-ach-payments/creating-a-payments-customer). If you recognize the buyer, pass in their existing Meld `customerId` when making the transaction.
3. **Payin** — With a Meld customer for both the seller and the buyer, make an ACH Payin by following the [Payin steps](/docs/payments-stack/fiat-payment-quickstart/making-ach-payments/ach-payin-debit).
4. **Payout** — Similarly, make an ACH Payout by following the [Payout steps](/docs/payments-stack/fiat-payment-quickstart/making-ach-payments/ach-credit-payout).
5. **Cashout** — Moving money from your account with the service provider to your bank account follows the same flow as an ACH Payout. You can schedule these to run automatically.

<Note>
For the lifecycle of an ACH transaction and the meaning of each status, see [ACH Transaction Statuses](/docs/payments-stack/fiat-payment-quickstart/making-ach-payments/ach-transaction-statuses).
</Note>
