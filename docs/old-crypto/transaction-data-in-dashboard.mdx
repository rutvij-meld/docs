---
title: "Transaction Data in Dashboard"
---

You will find a list of all transactions made through your Meld All in One wizard in your Meld dashboard, under the Transactions tab. The transactions are ordered by most recent first but you can filter them by date range, service provider, and status.

![](https://files.readme.io/2c1ba3a-image.png)

You can also export your transaction data in CSV format to store it locally.

# Analytics

Besides basic transaction data, you will also find more advanced data under the Analytics tab. This will allow you to know which onramps / markets / tokens might be performing better than others.

![](https://files.readme.io/806c722-image.png)
