title: "Step 5: Integration"
---

Now that you have finished setting up your service provider credentials, webhooks, and have played around with Meld's API, you are ready to integrate with Meld! Follow the steps found in the quickstart guide of the Meld Stack that you plan to use.

Note that if you start with a Meld sandbox account, when you receive a Meld production account, you must repeat the above steps, as they are per account.

[Creating Connections](https://docs.meld.io/docs/creating-connections#/)
