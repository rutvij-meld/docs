---
title: "Step 1: Accept Your Dashboard Invite"
---

Once Meld has created a dashboard account and invited you, check your email and accept the invite.

![](https://files.readme.io/19eb906-image.png)

If this is the first Meld account you have been invited to, you will have to set a password. If this is not your first Meld account, you can just click the link and log in with your existing password. You can see your account(s) in the dropdown in the top left.
