---
title: "Widget Customization"
---

Meld offers the ability to customize several UI elements of your meld connect widget through the Dashboard, in the Developer tab. You can customize the following options:

* **Font** -- This is the font shown for all text within the Meld picker

* **Institution Select Text** -- This is the text at the top of the Meld picker

![](https://files.readme.io/c949e2c-image.png)

These values are prepopulated by defaults, and you can restore your previous values by pressing the **Reset** button.

You can also customize the colors of the Routing Retry button colors. To do so, reach out to Meld for assistance.

![](https://files.readme.io/4aa95cf-image.png)
