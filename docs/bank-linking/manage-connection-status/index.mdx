---
title: "Manage Connections"
---

Over its lifetime, a connection may need to be managed in order to repair or otherwise change it. In this section you can find out how to:

1. [Refresh a connection](https://docs.meld.io/docs/refreshing-connections), either actively (forced) or passively (automatic). See [here](https://docs.meld.io/docs/refreshing-connections#how-to-refresh-a-connection) for an example.
2. [Update a connection.](https://docs.meld.io/docs/updating-connections) This allows you to add BALANCES as a product even after the connection has been made.
3. [Repair a connection.](https://docs.meld.io/docs/repairing-connections) This is when a connection isn't getting updated and likely needs user intervention (such as logging in again) to once again become ACTIVE.
4. [Delete a connection.](https://docs.meld.io/docs/deleting-connections) Sometimes users close bank accounts, or a connection may be a duplicate, or the provider will stop supporting an institution. All these and more are good reasons to delete a connection. See [here](https://docs.meld.io/docs/deleting-connections#how-to-delete-a-connection) for an example.
