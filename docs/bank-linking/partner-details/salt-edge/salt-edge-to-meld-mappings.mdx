## Financial Account Base Fields

This data comes from SaltEdge's [/api/v5/accounts](https://docs.saltedge.com/account_information/v5/#accounts-attributes) endpoint and is returned from Meld's [/bank-linking/accounts](https://docs.meld.io/reference/bank-linking-accounts-search) endpoint.
