---
title: "Finicity Investment Transactions Mapping"
---

This data comes from Finicity's [/investments/transactions/get](https://api-reference.finicity.com/#/rest/models/structures/transactions) endpoint and is returned from Meld's [/bank-linking/investments/transactions](https://docs.meld.io/reference/bank-linking-investment-transactions-search) endpoint.

<Note>
  Example values: `symbol: "AAPL"`, `quantity: 5`, `amount: -912.50`, `currency: "USD"`, `description: "BUY AAPL"`, `status: "POSTED"`, `transactionDate: "2024-08-09"`, `postedDate: "2024-08-10"`, `type: "BUY"`.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Finicity does not provide `cashBalance` for investment transactions.
</Warning>
