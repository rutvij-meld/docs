title: "Salt Edge Partners Transactions Mapping"

This data comes from Salt Edge Partners' [api/partners/v1/transactions](https://docs.saltedge.com/partners/v1/#transactions-list) endpoint and is returned from Meld's [/bank-linking/transactions](https://docs.meld.io/reference/bank-linking-transactions-search) endpoint.

<Note>
  Example values: `amount: -22.40`, `currency: "EUR"`, `status: "POSTED"`, `transactionDate: "2024-08-10"`, `postedDate: "2024-08-11"`.
</Note>
